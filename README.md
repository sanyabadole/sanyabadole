## 🧬 About Me

I'm an MS Bioinformatics student at **Georgia Institute of Technology** (GPA: 3.77, graduating May 2026) with industry experience in pharma R&D and computational biology. My work sits at the intersection of **single-cell genomics, multi-omics integration, immunology, and drug discovery**.

- 🏢 Former **Digital Transformation Intern @ Boehringer Ingelheim** — built production bioinformatics platforms on OpenShift/AWS with CI/CD
- 🔬 Researching **GBM tumor immune microenvironments** via scRNA-seq, scATAC-seq, VDJ-seq & spatial transcriptomics
- 💊 Building **RxBiome** — a pharmacomicrobiomics-driven drug discovery platform
- 🛠️ Developing an **LLM-powered VS Code extension** for reproducible bioinformatics workflows
- 🌍 Open to full-time roles in **bioinformatics, computational biology, or drug discovery** starting May 2026
